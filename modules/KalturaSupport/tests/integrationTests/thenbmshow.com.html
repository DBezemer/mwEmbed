Become a part of this free, open forum dialog where you have a chance to interact openly with a panel of awards & engraving industry experts representing a range of disciplines and perspectives. Here’s a chance to interact with your peers, tap the experience of specialists and put your finger on the pulse of the industry. Issues of immediate interest to awards & engraving shops will be addressed, including hot-button issues, technical questions and market strategies.
Topics could include…

  • Marketing strategies to keep your business growing
  • Advances in laser engraving
  • Niche markets for rotary engraving
  • Sublimation & full-color printing opportunities
  • Sandcarving opportunities
  • Mastering the awards sales cycle

Plus, bring your own questions and ideas to discuss with the participants. Panelists will vary by city, but will include a mix of seasoned and veteran practitioners as well as consultants, manufacturers’ reps, A&E Magazine contributors, and class instructors.
